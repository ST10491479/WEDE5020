Our Story

From Farmers' Market to Local Staple

The Daily Loaf started in 2012 as a small stall at the Christchurch Farmers' Market. Every Saturday, we woke up at 3am to bake sourdough in a shared kitchen and sold out by 10am. People kept asking where they could find our bread during the week.

In 2015, we opened our first brick-and-mortar shop on Baker Street. Today, we bake seven days a week and still use the same slow-fermentation method that made our sourdough famous.

Our Mission

To craft exceptional, wholesome baked goods that bring our community together. We use traditional methods and the finest local ingredients—no shortcuts, no preservatives.

Our Vision

To be the heart of the neighbourhood's daily rituals, known for quality, warmth, and sustainability.
